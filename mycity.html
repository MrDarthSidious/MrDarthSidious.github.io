Population:

Education:

    - March 14
    London Ontario's Education consists of 4 school boards

    - Thames Valley District School Board
    - London District Catholic School Board
    - Conseil Scolaire Viamonde
    - Conseil Scolaire Catholique Providence

  • London is also home to more than 20 private schools
  • London is home to London Central High School, one of the highest ranking schools in Ontario.
  • London is home to The University of Western Ontario(UWO) and Fanshawe College
    • Posted by: Jiaqi Deng

Transportation

    - March 14
  • London is connected to 401 which connects to Toronto and Windsor.
  • London is connected to 402 which connects to sarnia and 401
  • London has a public Transport system with 44 bus routes
  • London has 330 km of cycling paths
  • London is on the Canadian National Railway main line between Toronto and Chicago.
  • London national airport provides direct flights to Toronto, Ottwa, Vancouver and many other places.
    • Posted by: Jiaqi Deng

Sports

    - March 14
