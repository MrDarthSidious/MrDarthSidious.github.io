How to make Instant Noodles

A Recipe by: Tom Han



Instant Noodles are cheap, easy-to-prepare, and delicious meal. Though questionable in nutrtious value, Instant Noodles are one of the most widely eaten meals in the world.


    Ingredients:
  • The Noodles
  • Soup Mix
  • Flavor Powder


How to Prepare: Fill a pot with water and turn on the heat to max. When the pot starts bubbling, add the noodles, powder, and soup mix. Stir for 5 minutes and enjoy your steaming hot delicious bowl of instant noodles
